Why BusDine?

More Orders

Delivering multiple food orders to multiple city locations is highly inefficient for restaurants with less drivers. With BusDine, a particular restaurant can deliver multiple orders at one location (bus stop). This reduces the need to have a high amount of drivers as well as increases the capacity of orders which the restaurant can prepare and deliver.

How BusDine works?

Passenger shares bus booking number and orders a meal.

Restaurant receives the food order along with the passenger's live location.

Passenger live location notifies restaurant of bus location so delivery driver can leave.

Order and enjoy a tasty meal on the bus.
